How to Play

Master these simple rules and dominate the deck.

1

Play Higher or Equal

Play a card with the same rank or higher than the top of the discard pile.

2

Draw to 3

Draw cards until you have 3 in hand when allowed. Manage your resources wisely.

3

Master Special Cards

2 resets the stack, 7 clears it completely, Joker clears and gives you an extra turn.

4

Win Through Strategy

Play your hand cards, then face-up cards, then face-down (invalid picks up the stack!).
